# Availability of data and materials

Data from this study are not publicly available for ethical reasons, but are available from the first author on reasonable request. A list of study sites, a description of data management procedures and details of data analysis can likewise be obtained on request. All data is stored safely, in fire and theft-proof space, and on encrypted server or separate media. The key linking participants to data is stored separately. All data will be stored for 10 years to enable review.

# Authors’ contributions

LB is the project leader and principal investigator of the project. KG is the responsible physician. LB, KG, AP, MB and KH were all involved in research design. LB collected the data and LB, KG, AP, MB and KH will all be involved in the analytical work. LB prepared the first draft of this manuscript and all other authors contributed. All authors have reviewed and approved the final version.

# Ethics approval and consent to participate

The study has obtained ethical approval from the Regional Ethical Review Board at the University of Gothenburg, Sweden, with the reference number 234-16. Informed written consent was obtained from all participant PHCC managers, participants in the focus group interviews, and participant patients. Written and oral information stressed that participation was voluntary and could be stopped without consequences. Data are kept and handled according to safety standards. Analyses are done at the group level, and results are presented so that individuals cannot be recognized.
